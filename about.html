At Arusk Pharma, we are a young and dynamic pharmaceutical trading company specializing in the distribution of high-quality Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) to pharmaceutical manufacturers & research institutions worldwide. With a relentless commitment to excellence and a customer-centric approach, we strive to be your preferred partner for all your API sourcing needs.

Why Choose us?

Extensive API Portfolio: We offer a comprehensive selection of APIs covering diverse therapeutic categories. Our extensive portfolio allows pharmaceutical manufacturers and in short we thrives to provide a range of APIs under one roof.

Quality Assurance: Quality is the cornerstone of our business. We work closely with reputed manufacturers and suppliers to ensure that all our APIs adhere to stringent quality control measures and regulatory requirements as per ICH guidelines.

Global Reach: With a well-established network of partners and logistics capabilities, we have a global presence that enables us to serve clients across the world efficiently. We are well-equipped to facilitate international shipments and timely deliveries. Our key markets are Europe, CIS, Latam, Bangladesh, and the Middle East.

Regulatory Compliance: We understand the critical importance of compliance in the pharmaceutical industry. Our team is well-versed in international regulations, and we ensure that all our products meet the necessary quality and safety standards.

Customer-Focused Service: Our customers' success is our top priority. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional customer service, understanding our client's unique needs, and offering tailored solutions to meet their specific requirements.

Research and Development Support: We recognize the significance of research and development in advancing healthcare. As a proactive partner, we provide support to research institutions and pharmaceutical companies in sourcing APIs for their innovative projects
